### Current state:

#### `atrium-page-classification/small_data_samples/`
- **~245 page-image PNGs** (the only ARUP/B-derived content here), organized into 11 classification category folders: `DRAW` (68), `DRAW_L` (4), `LINE_HW` (8), `LINE_P` (41), `LINE_T` (5), `PHOTO` (17), `PHOTO_L` (6), `TEXT` (24), `TEXT_HW` (12), `TEXT_P` (40), `TEXT_T` (4).
- Source prefixes mix internal/derived material:
  - `presentation_thesis_*` - thesis defense presentation - may contain very small page scans inserted into the slides
  - `caa_conference-*` - CAA conference presentation - may contain very small page scans inserted into the slides
  - `thesis-*` - master's thesis itself - may contain very small page scans inserted into the figures
  - `ijdl-*` - IJDL paper based on the thesis - may contain very small page scans inserted into the figures
  - `guide_troja-*` - self-made guide for student newcomers to the Troja dormitory
  - `atrium-*` - ATRIUM project definition (ATRIUM-D1.1-DMP-vn.1 PDF document)
  - `pages_online_*` - about 50 samples of the CC BY-NC 4.0 license type images that were found online (mostly typewritten and handwritten document scans)
  - `ticket-*` - outdated train ticket

#### `atrium-alto-postprocess/data_samples/`
- 3 pseudo-source ALTO XML scans (`CTX000000001–3`) plus per-page splits under `PAGE_ALTO/` (7 files).
- Derived OCR text per page: `PAGE_TXT/`, `PAGE_TXT_LLM/`, `PAGE_TXT_LR/` (7 files each).
- Line-categorization / stats CSVs: `DOC_LINE_CATEG`, `DOC_LINE_CATEG_gpt`, `DOC_LINE_STATS`, `DOC_LINE_STATS_gpt`, plus page-stats CSVs.
- **Names and locations mentioned in these documents were invented from scratch - these are not real source scanned document samples from ARUP/B but only a synthetic copy of those.** 

**RESOLVED** to contain no real data samples

#### `atrium-nlp-enrich/data_samples/`
- Same 3 pseudo-source ALTO XML scans + line-categ CSVs.
- Derived enrichment outputs: `NE/`, `UDP/`, `UDP_NE/`, `TEITOK/` TEI XML, keyword CSVs (`KW_PER_DOC_*`, `keywords_summary_*`), NE/summary CSVs, and `paradata/` JSON logs.

**RESOLVED** to contain no real data samples

#### `atrium-translator/data_samples/`
- **16 source ARUP/B documents** under `my_documents/` (`C-*.xml`, `MTX201501307_anon.alto.xml`).
  - `C-*.xml` are loaded from AMCR URLs
  - `MTX201501307_anon.alto.xml` is an anonymized version of `MTX201501307.alto.xml` ([details](https://github.com/ufal/atrium-translator/blob/test/data_samples/README.md))
- Corresponding English translations + per-doc translation logs under `translated_files/` (32 files) and `paradata/` JSON.
