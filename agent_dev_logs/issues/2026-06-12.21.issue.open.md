## CC BY-NC vs. CC BY-NC-SA Licenses

* **CC BY-NC 4.0 (Attribution-NonCommercial):** This license allows others to remix, adapt, and build upon your work non-commercially. While they must acknowledge you and keep their work non-commercial, they do *not* have to license their derivative works under the same terms.
* **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 (Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike):** This license allows others to remix, adapt, and build upon your work non-commercially, but **they must license their new creations under the identical terms**.

Choosing the "SA" version ensures that any future models, datasets, or adaptations built upon your data remain freely available for non-commercial use and cannot be locked behind more restrictive proprietary licenses.
