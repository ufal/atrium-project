## 🎯 Goal
Define a benchmark and evaluate out-of-the-box Vision-Language Models (VLMs/LLMs) for holistic "document understanding" on our specific archaeological historical data, and compare them against the legacy ABBYY+ALTO+NLP-enrich pipeline.

## 📅 Hard constraints
- Models must ideally be open-weight and locally runnable for data sovereignty and exact paradata logging.
- Heavy multimodal models should not be used uniformly across millions of pages due to computational/financial unfeasibility. 

## 📦 Artifacts produced (in thread)
